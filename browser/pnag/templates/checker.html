ASO-Checker

ASO-Checker is an accompanying tool to MASON which can be used to check different attributes of a pre-selected ASO. The tool takes ASO sequences and checks for several important sequence- and target-species related attributes. Similar to the MASON algorithm, it screens for off-targets and evaluates whether the sequence is suitable for further use.
